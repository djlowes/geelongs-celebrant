My Role

Understand who you are as a couple

I’d be happy and honoured to meet, obligation free, to discuss a personalised wedding ceremony with you. Making your special day and wonderful is my goal as a celebrant. I will endeavour to capture the essence of you as a couple and make it your day.

Legal Documentation and formalities

I will complete all legal documentation and solemnise and register your marriage.

Prepare your ceremony

Including discussing all aspects with no limit to email, phone calls and chats in person. I'm very flexible in regards to your choices.

Conduct a rehearsal

I am here for you for your rehearsal dinner and am committed to making sure your special day goes perfectly.

On the day

On your wedding day I provide my own sound equipment, table & chair and your Certificate

My Services

I am a licenced and registered marriage celebrant in Geelong. I can also perform funeral services and host events.
