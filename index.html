Rosemary Friend - Geelong's Celebrant

I am a registered marriage celebrant operating in the Geelong and Surf Coast area. I am known for my calm nature, humour and excellent organisation. I'm very flexible and absolutely committed to making your wedding wonderful. I endeavour to capture the essence of you as a couple and make it your day. I’d be happy to meet obligation free to discuss a personalised wedding ceremony with you.

My Role

Understand who you are as a couple

I’d be happy and honoured to meet you without obligation to discuss a personalised wedding ceremony with you. Making your special day and wonderful is my goal as a celebrant.

Legal Documentation and formalities

I will complete all legal documentation and solemnise and register your marriage.

Prepare your ceremony

Including discussing all aspects with no limit to email, phone calls and chats in person. I'm very flexible in regards to your choices.

Conduct a rehearsal

I am here for you for your rehearsal and am committed to making sure your special day goes perfectly.

On the day

On your wedding day I provide my own sound equipment, table & chair and your Certificate
